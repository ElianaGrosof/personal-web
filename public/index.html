About Me

I recently graduated from Oberlin College with a double major in Computer Science and Politics. I am seeking job opportunities as a data analyst or data scientist, preferably in New York City.

I am committed to making the world a better place through the power of data. I have analyzed everything from political voting patterns to computer science educational practices. You can find examples of my work under my Projects and Blog sections.

I have a strong technical background, with extensive experience using Java, Python, R, and SQL. My social science background informs my analytics work, enabling me to ask the right questions and understand the significance of trends in the data.

I am always looking to learn something new - whether it is about a new way to visualize data or how European privacy regulation works.

In my spare time, I love to swing dance, rock climb, travel, and experience musical, visual, and performance art.
